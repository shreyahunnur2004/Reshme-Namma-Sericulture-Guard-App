# 🌾 Reshme-Namma: Sericulture Guard App

## 📖 Overview
Reshme-Namma is an Android application designed to help sericulture farmers monitor silkworm rearing conditions. It analyzes temperature and humidity based on silkworm growth stages and provides real-time actionable advice to prevent crop loss.

---

## 🌾 Problem Statement
Karnataka is the silk capital of India, but silkworms are highly sensitive to environmental changes. Even a small variation in temperature or humidity can destroy an entire batch. Small-scale farmers lack access to advanced monitoring systems.

---

## 💡 Solution
This app acts as a digital supervisor where farmers can:
- Enter temperature and humidity manually
- Get intelligent suggestions based on silkworm growth stage (Instar)
- Monitor batches effectively

---

## 🔄 Features

- 🐛 Batch Tracking (date, breed)
- 🌡️ Climate Data Entry
- ⚠️ Smart Advice System
- ⏰ Cocoon Harvest Alerts
- 🎨 Color-coded Climate Indicator

---
